  • The samosa pizza is a combanation between a samosa and a pizza.



  • This pizza is very different and it hasn't been perfected till now.



  • This pizza "fills you up" after two slices and is perfect for a family of four or five.



  • There are 8 slices in all sized boxes.(ex. A medium sized box has 8 slices, so does a large) The only ting that changes it the width of the pizza in the slices.



  • The samosa pizza can stay hot for 30 mins when it is out of the oven and left outside, no re-heating required till the 30 mins are up.
